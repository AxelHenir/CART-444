A sense of organization

Organization is when everything I need is within reach. I like having an ordered workspace because it helps me think clearer. Being able to oganize a collection and have it look and feel organized to other people is my goal. To do this, I use boxes. Boxes have content, boundaries and a label.

Content is self-explanatory, it's simply what's in the box. Content will inform on the shape, weight and size of the metaphorical box. The boundary is the line which separates the content from the non-content. The boundary has the resolute and important job of distinguishing that which belongs to the collection. The label is a window into the box. The label will allow us to know what's inside without having to open the box.
